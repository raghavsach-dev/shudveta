Privacy Policy

INTRODUCTION

This Privacy Policy describes how Shudveta IT Solutions ("we," "our," or "us") collects, uses, stores, shares, and protects your information when you use the IPD Now mobile application ("App"). We are committed to protecting your privacy and handling your data with transparency and care.

Please read this Privacy Policy carefully to understand our practices regarding your personal information. By downloading, installing, or using the App, you acknowledge that you have read and understand this Privacy Policy and agree to the collection, use, and disclosure of your information as described.

1. INFORMATION WE COLLECT

1.1 Personal Information

We collect the following personal information during account creation and profile setup:

  • Full name
  • Email address (used for authentication via OTP)
  • Phone number (for contact and emergency purposes)
  • Date of birth (to calculate age for medical purposes)
  • Gender (for appropriate medical profiling)
  • Emergency contact details (name and phone number)

1.2 Medical Information

The App collects detailed medical history through the MedicalHistoryScreen, including:

  • Allergies (food, medication, and environmental)
  • Medical conditions (current and past diagnoses)
  • Medications (current prescriptions and dosages)
  • Surgical history (procedures with dates)
  • Family medical history (hereditary conditions)

1.3 Location Data

With your permission, we collect your precise geographic location (latitude/longitude coordinates) to:

  • Find nearby hospitals
  • Calculate distance to medical facilities
  • Facilitate ambulance dispatch in emergency situations

1.4 Documents

You may upload medical documents through the FileUploadScreen, including:

  • Medical reports
  • Prescriptions
  • Test results
  • Other healthcare-related documentation

1.5 Family Information

If you use our family features, we collect information about:

  • Family relationships (Mother, Father, Son, Daughter, etc.)
  • Family connections through shared family codes
  • Reciprocal relationship definitions

1.6 Device Information

We automatically collect certain information about your device, including:

  • Device type and model
  • Operating system and version
  • IP address
  • Unique device identifiers
  • Mobile network information

1.7 Usage Data

We collect information about how you interact with the App, including:

  • Features used and frequency of use
  • Time spent on different screens
  • Booking patterns
  • Navigation patterns

1.8 Camera and Photo Library

With your explicit permission, we access your device's:

  • Camera (for taking photos of medical documents)
  • Photo library (for selecting existing documents)

These permissions are only used when you initiate document upload actions.

5.5 Account Deletion

You have the right to request permanent deletion of your IPD Now account and associated data. Our account deletion process is designed to be secure, transparent, and compliant with data protection requirements.

5.5.1 Account Deletion Process

To delete your account, you must complete a secure verification process:

  • Email Verification: Enter your registered email address to initiate the deletion request
  • OTP Confirmation: Verify your identity using a 6-digit code sent to your email
  • Permanent Deletion: Once verified, your account and data will be permanently deleted according to our retention policy

5.5.2 Data That Will Be Permanently Deleted

Upon account deletion, the following data will be permanently removed from our active systems:

  • Personal profile information (name, email, phone number, date of birth, gender)
  • Complete medical history and health records
  • Dr. Healio chat conversations and AI interaction history
  • Appointment history and hospital booking records
  • Location history and hospital check-in data
  • User preferences and app settings
  • Uploaded medical documents and images stored on Cloudinary
  • Family connections and relationship mappings
  • Emergency contact information
  • Device information and usage analytics linked to your account

5.5.3 Data Retention Timeline

Our data deletion follows a structured timeline to ensure complete removal:

  • Immediate Deletion (Within 24 Hours): Personal profile data, medical history, chat conversations, and user-generated content are removed from active systems
  • Extended Deletion (Within 30 Days): Complete removal from backup systems, cached data, and redundant storage locations
  • Legal Compliance Period (Up to 90 Days): Some anonymized transaction logs may be retained for legal, security, and fraud prevention purposes as required by law

5.5.4 Data That May Be Retained

The following data may be retained after account deletion for legitimate business and legal purposes:

  • Anonymized Analytics: Usage statistics with all personal identifiers removed
  • Security Logs: System access logs required for security monitoring and fraud prevention
  • Legal Compliance: Data required by law for regulatory compliance, audit trails, or ongoing legal proceedings
  • Service Integrity: Information necessary to prevent abuse, maintain service security, or protect other users

5.5.5 Third-Party Data Deletion

We will coordinate with our service providers to ensure deletion of your data:

  • Firebase: Account data, medical records, and family relationships will be deleted from Firestore
  • Cloudinary: All uploaded medical documents and images will be permanently deleted
  • Google Services: Chat history with Dr. Healio and location data shared with Google Places API will be removed
  • Airtable: Emergency dispatch records and ambulance coordination data will be deleted

5.5.6 Verification and Confirmation

Our account deletion process includes verification steps to protect your data:

  • Email-based OTP verification ensures only the account owner can request deletion
  • Clear warnings about the permanent nature of account deletion
  • Confirmation notification upon successful account deletion
  • Option to download your data before deletion (where technically feasible)

5.5.7 Consequences of Account Deletion

Please understand the permanent consequences of account deletion:

  • Irreversible Process: Account deletion cannot be undone
  • Data Recovery: Deleted data cannot be recovered or restored
  • Service Access: You will lose access to all IPD Now services and features
  • Family Connections: All family member connections will be removed
  • Medical History: All stored medical information will be permanently lost
  • Future Use: You may create a new account with the same email after deletion, but all previous data will be gone

5.5.8 Support and Assistance

If you need help with account deletion:

  • Contact our support team at privacy@shudveta.in for assistance
  • We can guide you through alternative options if account deletion isn't necessary
  • Technical support is available if you encounter issues during the deletion process
  • We will confirm completion of the deletion process within 30 days
