IPD Now

A comprehensive hospital resource management platform providing real-time information for patients and healthcare providers.

Transforming Healthcare Access

IPD Now is our flagship initiative designed to bridge the critical gap in healthcare information accessibility. We're building a platform that empowers patients to find the right care at the right time, while enabling healthcare providers to optimize their resources and deliver better patient experiences.

Our solution tackles one of the most pressing challenges in healthcare today: the lack of real-time information about hospital resources, leading to inefficiencies, delays, and suboptimal patient experiences.

The Problems We're Solving

Bed Availability Uncertainty

Patients and families often face uncertainty when trying to find available hospital beds, leading to delays in critical care and unnecessary stress during emergencies.

Inefficient Referral Systems

The current patient referral process between hospitals is often manual and time-consuming, creating bottlenecks and potentially compromising patient care.

Resource Allocation Challenges

Healthcare facilities struggle to optimize resource allocation without real-time data and predictive analytics, leading to inefficiencies and increased operational costs.

Our Solution

Real-Time Resource Tracking

IPD Now provides real-time visibility into hospital bed availability, equipment status, and staffing levels. Patients can quickly find facilities with available resources, while hospitals can monitor and manage their capacity efficiently.

AI-Driven Patient Assistance

Our intelligent chatbot helps patients navigate healthcare options, providing guidance on finding appropriate facilities based on their needs, insurance coverage, and proximity. It offers 24/7 support for common queries and emergency guidance.

Emergency Response Coordination

During emergencies, IPD Now facilitates rapid coordination between emergency services and hospitals, directing ambulances to facilities with available capacity and appropriate specializations.

Hospital Management Dashboard

Healthcare administrators gain access to comprehensive analytics and management tools, helping them optimize resource allocation, staff scheduling, and patient flow based on historical data and predictive insights.
